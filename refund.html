Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Setup Fees

  • Before work begins: Full refund available
  • After work begins: Partial refund based on work completed
  • After delivery: No refund (service already provided)

2. Monthly Services

  • Monthly fees are non-refundable once the billing period starts
  • Cancel anytime with 15 days notice to stop future billing
  • No charges for months after cancellation takes effect

3. Hardware Services

For laptop/PC repair and data recovery:

  • Diagnosis is free - no charge if we can't fix the issue
  • Data recovery: No data recovered = No charge
  • 30-day warranty on all repairs

4. How to Request Refund

Contact us via WhatsApp with:

  • Your name and service details
  • Reason for refund request
  • Payment reference if available

5. Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.
