Common Questions

How long does it take to receive my refund?

Once approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. Credit card refunds may take up to 10 business days to appear on your statement.

Will I receive the refund to the same payment method?

Yes, all refunds are processed to the original payment method. If that's not possible, we'll work with you to find an alternative solution.

What if my refund request is denied?

We'll explain the reason and offer alternatives like service credits, extended support, or additional revisions to address your concerns.

Can I get store credit instead?

Yes! If you'd prefer store credit, let us know. We'll add the credit to your account for future services or products.
